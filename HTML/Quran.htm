What is Quran?

It is the Holy book of Islam- A guidance to the Mankind. Quran was revealed by Allah to the final Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ), through Angel Jibril, incrementally over a period of 23 years. Shortly after the Prophet's death, the Quran was compiled by the companions, who had written down or memorized it. Quran has been preserved and unchanged till this day and will be in future too. There are 114 surahs in the Quran, each divided into ayahs (verses). The chapters or surahs are of unequal length; the shortest chapter (Al-Kawthar) has only three verses while the longest (Al-Baqara) contains 286 verses. Of the 114 chapters in the Quran, 86 are classified as Meccan, while 28 are Medinan. The Quran is perhaps the only book, that has been memorized completely by millions of people.
