3.Zakat (Charity)

Shahadah, is the third pillar of Islam. Muslims bear witness to the oneness of God by reciting the creed, “There is no true God worthy of worship but God and Muhammad is the last and final Messenger of God.” This is simple yet profound statement expresses a Muslim’s complete acceptance of and total commitment to Islam.
